Examples of Exhibition works

Our summer exhibition this year will be at Prickwillow Engine Museum on Friday 24th July until Friday 31st, 2020.

Please note that all works that are not marked 'NFS' can be bought and if sold indicated as such with a red dot. The works can be collected at the end of the exhibition.

All members who wish to exhibit must provide details of their work:

  • Their membership name (to be linked to their club biography)
  • Title of the work
  • Media used (pastel, acrylic, oils, mixed, etc.)
  • Dimensions in centimeters
  • An image of the work attached to their submission email

Please ensure that your work is dry in time for the exhibition!

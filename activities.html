Activities

What are we up to?.

Working in graphite, charcoal, pastel, inks, acrylics, oils and three dimensional materials, our members have a vast range of skills and experience to draw from.

We will be providing talks, demonstrations and get-togethers throughout the year, with the main event being the summer exhibition.
